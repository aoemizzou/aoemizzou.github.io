About Us


  • The Beta Zeta chapter of Alpha Omega Epsilon was founded on April 21, 2012 at University of Missouri - Columbia.

  • Who Are We?

    We are a professional and social sorority composed of females in engineering and technical sciences.

  • Recruitment

    A.O.E. is open to all female students in any engineering discipline as well as other technical sciences.
